## Dental Cleaning

### Original 🤔
I went to the dentist for the first time in two months to get my teeth cleaned.  
The plaque is probably gone, but my gums are stinging from the thorough polishing.  
Anyway, there is a president who is great age, but has pearly white teeth.  
 I suppose he spent a fortune, but unnatural.  

### Corrected 🤖
I went to the dentist for the first time in two months to get my teeth cleaned.  
The plaque is probably gone, but my gums are stinging from the thorough polishing.  
Anyway, there's a president who's quite old, but he's got really white teeth.  
I suppose he spent a lot of money, but it doesn't seem right.  

### Memo (dum!)
教訓：白い歯の老人に政治を託してはいけない. 
🤖 Moral of the story: Never entrust politics to an old man with white teeth.  

### Picked Phrase
- for the first time in two months   くどい言い回しだけれど英語ではこういう　🤖  It’s a bit of a clunky way of putting it, but that’s how you say it in English.
