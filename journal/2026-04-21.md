## Books for the Elderly

### Original 🤔
I’m listening to an essay written by a 91 years old English literature scholar on Audible.  
He, Toyama Shigehiko said interesting thing, so I’m writing it down here.  
"For the Elderly, books that bring a small or a tear are enough, because both are necessary for them.  
Don’t read books that are supposed to be useful. There’re no time left to put them to use.”  
But I can’t be that philosophical about it now.  

### Corrected 🤖
I'm listening to an essay written by a 91-year-old English literature expert on Audible.  
I'm writing down something that Toyama Shigehiko said, because it was interesting.  
"For the Elderly, books that make them laugh or cry are enough, because both are necessary for them.  
Don't read books that are supposed to be useful. There's no time left to use them."  
But I can't think about it in such depth right now.
