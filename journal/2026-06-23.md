## A Present Thief?

### Original 🤔
Last Friday was my granddaughter's birthday, so I ordered it on Amazon to make sure it arrived on the day itself.  
I received a message with a photo confirming that the delivery was complete, but it seems someone stole it from the mailbox afterward.  
Even a thief would be disappointed if it was just a kids' puzzle, right? Or maybe they'll resell it to make some money?  
It was four days late, but I reordered it and sent it.
