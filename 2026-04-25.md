### Corrected 🤖
I can’t read the content, but while browsing the BBC website, I came across an article titled “No. 10 says Falklands sovereignty rests with UK.”  
Does this mean it’s a statement from the 10th most important person in the country, after the Prime Minister (or maybe the King)?  
Even when I translate it with DeepL, it still only shows “No. 10.”  
I knew it was a class-based society, but this is really tricky.  
I looked it up in a dictionary. The British Prime Minister’s official residence is “10 Downing Street, London.” This address is also used to refer to the government as a whole.  
Ah, so it’s like Nagatacho!  
I’ve rediscovered the joy of studying foreign languages.
