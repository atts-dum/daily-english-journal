## My Reading Habits

### Original 🤔
Reading is one of my hobbies, and also part of my bedtime habits.  
I used to read a book 20 pages and more, sometimes I could’t sleep.  
Recently I read only a few pages and next day I forget what I’ve read.  
It’s useful as a hypnotic, but I’m reading about five books at once, I can’t seem to finish them.  

### Corrected 🤖
Reading is one of my hobbies and part of my bedtime routine.  
I used to read over 20 pages at times, but that would sometimes keep me awake.  
Lately, I read a few pages only to forget what I read the next day.  
It has a hypnotic effect, but since I'm reading five books at once, I can't seem to finish any of them.
