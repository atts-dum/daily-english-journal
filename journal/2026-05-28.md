## The Piano Has Arrived 

### Original 🤔
Yesterday evening, the piano Y had been eagerly awaiting arrived at our house.  
After dinner, she played the piano for nearly three hours. (She wore headphones so as not to disturb anyone.). 
When I saw it, I understood why a keyboard just wasn't enough.  
Now that the living room has become a practice room, maybe I should replace the old air conditioner?  

### Corrected 🤖
The piano that Y had been looking forward to arrived at our house yesterday evening.  
After dinner, she played the piano for nearly three hours. (She wore headphones so as not to disturb anyone).  
When I saw it, I understood why a keyboard just wasn't enough.  
Now that the living room is being used as a practice room, maybe I should replace the old air conditioner?
