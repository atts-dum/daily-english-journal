## To the Weekend Clinic

### Original 🤔
I had a headache when I woke up this morning, so I went to the weekend clinic.  
To be precise, I woke up several times because of a headache.  
I thought it was because of the pillow, but Y was worried I might have had a stroke.  
The diagnosis was swollen tonsils, but I immediately ordered a pillow online.  

### Corrected 🤖
I woke up this morning with a headache, so I went to the clinic over the weekend.  
To be precise, the headache woke me up several times.  
I thought it was because of my pillow, but Y was worried it might be a stroke.  
The diagnosis was swollen tonsils, but I immediately ordered a new pillow online.
