## A turtle on the road

### Original 🤔
I always take the levee road to get to work.  
The day before yesterday, I was paying close attention because the car in front of me was slowing down, and I saw a turtle crossing the road.  
It about the size of a rugby ball had crossed from the river toward the houses.  
I don’t know what was going on, but it was a very strange sight.  
The river was swollen today because of the typhoon, but maybe it had evacuated?  

### Corrected 🤖
I always take the route along the levee when I'm heading to work.  
The day before yesterday, I was paying close attention because the car in front of me was slowing down, and I saw a turtle crossing the road.  
It was about the size of a rugby ball and had crossed from the river towards the houses.  
I'm not sure what was going on, but it was a strange sight.  
The river was swollen today because of the typhoon, but maybe it had evacuated?
