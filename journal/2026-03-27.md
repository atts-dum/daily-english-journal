## Back to Siberia?

### Original 🤔
While waiting for my packages to arrive at the delivery center, I saw geese flying toward Siberia.  
It was a V-formation flight. Has spring finally arrived in Siberia?  
AI said some geese stay in Japan, if it was me, I wouldn’t go north.  
That is the harsh place where my father was interned 80 years ago.  

### Corrected 🤖
While I was waiting for packages to arrive at the distribution center, I spotted a flock of geese flying toward Siberia.  
They were flying in a V-formation. I wonder if spring has finally arrived in Siberia?  
According to AI, it seems some of the geese will stay in Japan. If it were me, I wouldn’t go north.  
That is the harsh place where my father was interned 80 years ago.

V字サイン、吉兆だ
🤖 The V sign is a good omen.
