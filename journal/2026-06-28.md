## Wild Birds vs. Y

### Original 🤔
Y has been really into growing blueberries for the past few years.  
The year before last was a bumper crop, but last year was a poor harvest.  
A wild bird flew over and pecked at it—that’s the third time I’ve seen that today.  
While she blamed me for not chasing them away, she was using the seat to create a barrier.  

### Corrected 🤖
Y's been obsessed with growing blueberries for years now.  
The year before last was great, but last year was a bust.  
Some wild birds came in and started pecking at the blueberries. This is the third time today that this has happened.  
She gave me a hard time for not chasing the birds away, but she was using a sheet to make a barrier.
