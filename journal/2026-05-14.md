## So sleepy!

### Original 🤔
I'm always the sleepiest just after 3 o'clock.  
If I drink coffee or take a nap, I can’t sleep at night.  
I think it’s because of the weather, I often find myself getting sleepy at work.  
Anyway, I should go to bed early.  

### Corrected 🤖
Around 3:00 p.m., that’s when I get the sleepiest.  
If I drink coffee or take a nap, I end up not being able to sleep at night.  
I think it’s because of the weather, but I used to doze off at work back in the day.  
Anyway, I really should go to bed early.
