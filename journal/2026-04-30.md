## Blood Test Results

### Original 🤔
I went to the doctor today to get the results of the blood test that 2 month ago.  
The doctor said “Improved, but barely within acceptable limits.”  
He told me to hang in there and gave me a prescription.  
I’ll have to keep taking the medication for another two months.  

### Corrected 🤖
I went to the doctor today to get the results of a blood test I had two months ago.  
The doctor said, "The situation has improved, but it's still not quite good enough."  
He told me to be patient and gave me a prescription.  
I'll have to continue taking the medication for two more months.
