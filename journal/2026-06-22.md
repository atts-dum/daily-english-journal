## Triple Trouble 

### Original 🤔
Today, I went to work with three things on my mind.  
To be precise, there are three types of pain. As usual, I have pain in my chest and head, but recently I’ve also started feeling pain in my side.  
As a result, delivery speeds have slowed down.  

### Corrected 🤖
Today, I went to work with three problems weighing on me.  
To be precise, they’re pains. As usual, I have pain in my chest and head, but recently I’ve also started feeling pain in my side.  
Because of that, my delivery speed has slowed down.  

### Memo (dum!)
マジでやばいかも　ボクの健康寿命は平均以下みたい  
🤖  This might be seriously bad—it looks like my healthy life expectancy is below average.  

### Picked Phrase
- feeling pain in my side　腹痛=胃痛になるのでこのように表現した　胃ではありません　 🤖  I used this expression because “abdominal pain” is often equated with “stomach pain”—but it's not actually the stomach.
