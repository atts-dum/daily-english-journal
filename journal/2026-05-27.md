## Hard to lose weight

### Original 🤔
My goal is to lose 1 kg every month, but it looks like it’s going to be tough this month.  
I’d like to lose at least 0.5 kg, but it might be tough.  
This morning, I weighed the most I have this month.  
I tend to gain weight in the summer, so I guess it can’t be helped.

My goal is to lose 1 kilogram every month, but it looks like it's going to be tough this month.

I tend to gain weight in the summer, so I guess there's nothing I can do about it.
