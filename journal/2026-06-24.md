## Near Miss 

### Original 🤔
While making a delivery today, I almost hit a bicycle that was coming up behind me as I turned right.  
That was a close call—my heart almost stopped (no joke).  
It seems it was fortunate that the person riding the bike was muscular and athletically gifted.  

### Corrected 🤖
Today, while I was driving, I almost hit a bike that was coming up behind me as I turned right.  
That was a close call – my heart almost stopped (no joke).  
Luckily, the person riding the bike was strong and good at sports.  

### Memo (dum!)
しかし事故起こしたら心臓止まるな  
🤖  But if I get into an accident, my heart’s gonna stop.  

### Picked Phrase
- The person riding the bike was strong and good at sports.    運動神経が良いという意味です   🤖  It means “to be athletically gifted.”
