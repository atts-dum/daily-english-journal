## A Walk After a Long Time

### Original 🤔
It was cloudy but not cold today, I went for a walk to a park 2km away.  
I think walking is the best way to relax.  
While listening to the music on my iPhone,  I went to see the cherry blossoms that were still in bud.  
For the past two weeks, I’ve been working on my tax return, and I was getting fed up with it.  

### Corrected 🤖
