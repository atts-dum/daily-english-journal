## What Was Lost from My Hometown

### Original 🤔
I grew up in Shimane, in the Sanin region, one of the most rural areas in Japan.  
People cannot make a living simply because the area has a long history, is unspoiled, or is quiet.  
There are other rural areas, but Shimane seems to lack vitality.  
They need jobs, but companies aren't moving in.  
It’s a shame we haven’t fostered this industry until now.  

### Corrected 🤖
I grew up in Shimane Prefecture, in the San’in region—one of the most rural areas in Japan.  
People can’t make a living simply because the region has a long history, remains undeveloped, or is quiet.  
There are other rural areas, but Shimane seems to lack vitality.  
They need jobs, but companies aren’t coming here.  
It’s a shame that They haven’t nurtured industries here over the years.
