## Odawara Castle Site

### Original 🤔
Yesterday, I went to Odawara on my way back from the clinic in Hiratsuka.  
I went to the castle—it might have been my first time in 30 years.  
Back then, there was a small zoo in the castle square that was said to be the worst in the world.  
The elephant was trapped in a small birdcage (from its perspective).  
It’s now beautifully maintained, and it seems they hold ninja shows there.  
It wasn't exactly a sentimental journey, but it did give me a taste of what traveling is like.  



### Corrected 🤖
Yesterday, I went to Odawara on my way back from the clinic in Hiratsuka.  
I went to the castle, and it might have been the first time I went there in 30 years.  
In the past, there was a small zoo in the castle square. People said it was the worst zoo in the world.  
The elephant was trapped in a small birdcage (from its perspective).  
It’s been beautifully restored now, and it looks like they’re holding ninja shows there.  
It wasn’t exactly a sentimental journey, but it did give me a taste of what traveling is like.  

### Memo (dum!)
 確かその象はストレスが高じて飼育員を踏みつけた　ひどい話だ  
🤖  That elephant was so stressed out that it trampled a zookeeper. What a terrible story.  

### Picked Phrase
- It might have been the first time I went there in 30 years.   2回目でも30年間では初回と表現するんだ  🤖  Even if it’s the second time, over a 30-year period, it’s still referred to as the first time.
