## The Start of the Rainy Season 

### Original 🤔
The weather bureau announced that the rainy season has begun.  
The next month is going to be full of damp days. Many people find it annoying, but I don’t really mind.  
I think it’s because I was born during this season. But more than that, since I’ve worked in disaster prevention, I pray that no disasters will occur.  

### Corrected 🤖
The Japan Meteorological Agency has announced that the rainy season has begun.  
We can expect a month of muggy weather ahead. While many people find it bothersome, it doesn’t bother me much.  
I think that’s because I was born during this season. But more than that, as someone who has worked in disaster prevention, I pray that no disasters occur.  

### Memo (dum!)
梅雨の終わりが要注意、大雨になり土砂災害が発生する  
🤖  Be on the lookout as the rainy season comes to an end; heavy rains may lead to landslides.  

### Picked Phrase
- We can expect a month of muggy weather ahead.    「これから1か月」なるほどこう書くんだ     🤖 “For the next month”—I see, so that's how I write it.
