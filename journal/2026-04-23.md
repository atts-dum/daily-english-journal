## Don't get complacent

### Original 🤔
It is often said that a series of near misses will occur a major accident.  
I missed the delivery customer again today.  
I make mistakes a few times a week.  
The cause is a lack of urgency and complacency.  

### Corrected 🤖
People often say, "Small mistakes can add up and lead to a major accident."  
Today, I drove past the delivery address again.  
I make mistakes several times a week.  
I don't pay enough attention and I'm too relaxed.  

### Memo (dum!)
