## Selling My Book Collection

### Original 🤔
On my way to the station, I once saw some books that had been put out for trash pickup.  
Since they were all old books, I thought someone had thrown away the books that belonged to the person who had passed away.  
I don’t want to put my family through that kind of trouble, so I decided to sell everything.  
There’s a really great app that scans barcode and lists them in Excel.  
It’s  handy for keep track of my collection (about 60% of which I haven’t read yet…) and for requesting quotes from used bookstores.
