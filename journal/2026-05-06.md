## The earthquake 15 minutes ago

### Original 🤔
I felt the tremor 15 minutes ago.  
The earthquake, with a magnitude of 4.2, had its epicenter in eastern Kanagawa and occurred at a considerable depth.  
For a split second, our house seemed to flip upside down.  
It’s been a while since the last earthquake, but I wasn’t scared at all.  
Every time there’s an earthquake, I wonder if the next one will be a big one.  

### Corrected 🤖
I felt an earthquake 15 minutes ago.  
It had a magnitude of 4.2, with the epicenter in eastern Kanagawa Prefecture; the hypocenter was quite deep.  
The house shook up and down.  
It had been a while since the last earthquake, but I wasn’t scared at all.  
Every time there’s an earthquake, I worry that the next one might be even bigger.
