## Going for a drive with Y

### Original 🤔
The weather felt very spring-like today, so Y and I went for a drive to the mountain said to buy the famous dumpling this afternoon.  
But unfortunately it was sold out, so We decided to take a drive to the beach.  
Of course, the drive was fun, but 24 hours later, I’ll go through this way for a delivery made me feel gloomy.   

### Corrected 🤖
The weather was very spring-like today, so Y and I drove to the mountain this afternoon to get those famous steamed buns they're known for.   
Unfortunately, they were all sold out, so we decided to drive to the beach instead.  
The drive itself was fun, but I felt a bit gloomy thinking about having to take the same route for a delivery in 24 hours.
