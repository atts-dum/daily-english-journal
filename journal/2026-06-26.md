## A Half-Baked Birthday Present 

### Original 🤔
Today is the game against Sweden and my birthday.  
Naturally, I expected the victory to be a present for me.  
I cheered Japan on from the morning, but the game ended in a tie.  
Damn it, I really wanted them to win!  
But come to think of it, Texas was the day before. Oh well.  

### Corrected 🤖
Today is the game against Sweden and my birthday.  
I mean, I was expecting the victory to be a gift from them.  
I was cheering for Japan from the morning on, but the game ended in a tie.  
Man, I really wanted them to win!  
But actually, Texas was the day before. Oh well.  

### Memo (dum!)
プレゼントは当日ほしいという前提でのオチです
🤖  The punchline is based on the assumption that I want the gift on the day itself.
