## Weekend Rebound

### Original 🤔
I started dieting in March, and I've been losing about 1 kg every month.  
Yesterday was a record low. I think my diet and delivery job really paid off.  
But the problem is the weekend—I gain weight every time.  
Well, I suppose it'll be the same this time.  

### Corrected 🤖
I started my diet in March and have been losing about a kilo a month.  
Yesterday was the coldest day on record. I think the combination of my diet and my delivery job has really paid off.  
But my weight always goes up on the weekends.  
I’m sure it’ll be the same this time, too.  

### Memo (dum!)
体重が戻ることを”リバウンド”といっているが、辞書によるとバスケットボールの用語ではあるが、ダイエット用語としては見当たらない  
🤖 People refer to regaining weight as a “rebound,” but according to the dictionary, while it is a term used in basketball, it does not appear in the context of dieting.  

### Picked Phrase
- Yesterday was a record low. → Yesterday was the coldest day on record.　本当に寒くなるような手直しだ  🤖 That’s a change that’s really going to make things cold.
