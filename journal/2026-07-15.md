## iPad Apps 

### Original 🤔
Generally speaking, iPad apps are intuitive and easy to use.  
But if I don’t use it for a few weeks, I pretty much forget how to do it.  
To that end, I study by watching YouTube or reading manual, but it feels like a waste of time.  
As the old saying goes, “Practice makes perfect,” so even if I can’t master all the features, it’s important to use it every day.  

### Corrected 🤖
