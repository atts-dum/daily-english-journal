Y is taking piano lessons to fulfill a childhood dream.  
I thought a small keyboard would be enough, but she actually went and bought a digital piano.  
Since it was expensive, she usually prefers to pay the full amount upfront, but this time she’s paying in installments. She used to keep a close eye on me to make sure I wasn’t secretly taking out a loan.  
Honestly, I was surprised. I’ll earn the money through my job and pay for it. I’d be happy to do it!
