## A Beagle's Escape

### Original 🤔
While making a delivery, I spotted a beagle relaxing by the side of the road.  
Having owned two beagles over the past 30 years, it clicked for me, It's definitely an escape!  
According to a neighbor, it always runs away but comes back home after about 30 minutes.  
Sakura, Kaede, I was always in a rush.
