## Like the early bird

### Original 🤔
I woke up earlier than the birds today.  
If you ask what I did, it was setting up my smartphone.  
The bird’s whisper at dawn is helpful, but my actions doesn’t help anyone.  
It’s bad for my health. I wasted my precious day off.  

### Corrected 🤖
I woke up earlier than the birds today.  
If you want to know what I did, I set up my smartphone.  
The bird's quiet song in the morning is helpful, but my actions aren't helping anyone.  
It's bad for my health. I wasted my precious day off.  

### Memo (dum!)
思い起こすと有意義な休日というものを過ごしたことはないな
🤖 Come to think of it, I don’t think I’ve ever had a truly meaningful day off.

### Picked Phrase
- but my actions aren't helping anyone.  前の文章を受けるとやっぱこれだよね  🤖  Given the previous sentence, this is indeed the expression that results.
