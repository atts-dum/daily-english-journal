## It’s raining

### Original 🤔
Long time no rain.  
Of all days, it just had to be the day with the most deliveries.  
Winter has come back again. Should I wear the winter gear or the rain gear today?  

### Corrected 🤖
It's been raining for the first time in a while.  
And of all days, it had to be the busiest day for deliveries.  
Winter has returned. Should I wear my winter clothes today, or should I wear my rain gear?  

### Memo (dum!)
梅雨の時期もあるから雨は仕方ないけれど、晴天続きでいきなりの雨には身構えてしまう  
🤖 Rain is unavoidable during the rainy season, but after days of clear skies, sudden showers make me brace myself.  

### Picked Phrase
- Of all days, it just had to be the day with　多くの”days”の中からこの日に限ってという意味になる訳ね　🤖  It means specifically this day out of all the days, right?
