Among the customers I deliver to, there is an elderly person who is a poor memory.  
Every time, she is too wary to open the door.

### Corrected
One of my clients is an elderly person who tends to be forgetful.  
Every time I visit, she is very wary and rarely opens the door for me.  
I got a little frustrated, and it seems my feelings showed on my face, which scared her.  
Starting today, I’m going to greet my clients with a smile!
