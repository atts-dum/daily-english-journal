# 2026-06-13
## The discomfort in my chest was severe.

### Original 🤔
I underwent a thorough examination at the cardiology department today and was diagnosed with angina.  
I was told to undergo catheter treatment within the next month or two.  
I had surgery for varicose veins in my legs last year, but it seems like one misfortune after another just keeps happening.  

### Corrected 🤖
I had a heart doctor check me out today, and they said I have angina.  
I was told to get a catheter treatment in the next couple of months.  
I had surgery for varicose veins in my legs last year, but it seems like one bad thing after another keeps happening.  


### Memo (dum!)
循環器内科の医者＝心臓専門医でどっちも”cardiologist”なんだ  
🤖 A doctor in the Department of Cardiology is, in other words, a heart specialist, and both terms seem to be rendered as “cardiologist.”  

### Picked Phrase
- it seems like one bad thing after another keeps happening.　こういう表現いいね　　  🤖  I like this kind of expression.
