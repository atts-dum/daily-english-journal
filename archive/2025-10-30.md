## The purpose of writing a journal

### Original
Why I write a journal every day? Of course do it for improving to English skills.  
But a journal is only tool, not purpose. So I should write a journal with honesty without hiding anything. Especially about financial matters are important, write them and I might solve the problem.  

### Corrected
Why do I write in a journal every day? Of course, to improve my English skills.  
However, a journal is only a tool, not a purpose. So, I should write in my journal honestly, without hiding anything. It's especially important to write about financial matters because doing so might help me solve the problem.
