## Results of follow-up examination

### Original 🤔
Today, Y went to the university hospital for a follow-up checkup after her breast cancer surgery.  
In the case of breast cancer, 10 years of follow-up are required, and this year marks the eighth year.  
Everything had been going smoothly until now, but this time there was an abnormality in the lymph nodes.  
She has a follow-up exam in three months.  
I hope that when I see the results of it, I’ll be able to smile at Y and say, “I’m so glad everything went well.”  

### Corrected 🤖
Today, Y went to the university hospital for a follow-up checkup after her breast cancer surgery.  
With breast cancer, follow-up care is required for 10 years, and this year marks her eighth year.  
There haven’t been any problems so far, but this time, an abnormality was found in her lymph nodes.  
She has a follow-up exam in three months.  
I hope that when I see the results, I’ll be able to smile at Y and say, “I’m so glad everything is going well.”
