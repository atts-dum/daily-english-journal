## Three types of honey

### Original 🤔
I eat a spoonful of honey that mix it with milk every morning.  
It’s a habit since my brother gave his made to me as a present.  
I always looked forward to his sending it, but last year he failed, so I’ve been eating Myanmar and New Zealand ones.  
I eat three types of honey and I only remember these sweetness.  
But I think my brother’s  is the best! I’m really hoping for this year!   

### Corrected 🤖
Every morning, I enjoy mixing a spoonful of honey into my milk.  
This was a gift my younger brother made for me.  
I always looked forward to him sending it, but I heard he failed last year, so instead I've been eating honey from Myanmar and New Zealand.  
I've tried three kinds of honey, but all I remember is their sweetness.  
Still, I think his honey is the tastiest! I'm really looking forward to this year.
