## UHF?

### Original 🤔
UHF stands for "Ultra-High Frequency".  
UHF waves travel in a straight line and can reach long distances if there are no obstacles.  
Recently, I have found it hard to stay calm. But since this quality of the waves matches my own personality, I've decided to call myself "Ultra-High Short-Tempered" (UHS).  

### Corrected 🤖
UHF stands for “Ultra-High Frequency.”  
UHF waves travel in a straight line and can reach long distances if there are no obstacles.  
Lately, I've been getting more and more short-tempered. However, since the nature of these waves resembles my own personality, I've decided to call myself “Ultra-High Short-Tempered” (UHS).
