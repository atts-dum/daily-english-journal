# 2026-07-09
## Sunshine

### Original 🤔
It’s the first sunny day in 20 days this morning.  
I was getting fed up with the rainy season, so I’m happy  
Since the clouds over Mt. Oyama had cleared, I decided to take a look through the telescope.  

### Corrected 🤖
It's been 20 days since the last sunny day.  
I was so over the rainy season, so I was stoked when the clouds over Mt. Oyama cleared. I decided to take a look through the telescope.  

### Memo (dum!)
オリジナルで“so I’m happy”のピリオドを忘れたら、文意が変わって大山が見えたことに喜んだみたいになった　ピリオドを付けるとボクの真意が反映された  
🤖  When I forgot to put a period after “so I’m happy” in the original text, the meaning changed, and it came across as if I was happy to have seen Mount Oyama. Once I added the period, my true intention was conveyed correctly.  

### Picked Phrase
- It’s the first sunny day in 20 days.  /  It's been 20 days since the last sunny day.    いずれにしても日本語なら一言、英語は多くの単語が必要      🤖 In any case, a single word is enough in Japanese, but English requires many words.
