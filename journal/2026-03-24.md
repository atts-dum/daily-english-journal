## Lazy me

### Original 🤔
This isn’t going well, in other words, I’m just lazy.  
I put off until tomorrow what I decided to do today.  
Particularly I’m not tired, but mental health issues?, really just aging?  
This is a bit bad.  

### Corrected 🤖
Things aren’t going well—or maybe I’m just being lazy.  
I always end up putting off until tomorrow the things I decided to do today.  
I’m not even tired, so is this a mental health issue? Or is it just a sign that I’m getting older?  
This isn’t good.
